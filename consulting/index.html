The Digital Exposure Audit

Somewhere right now, a people-search site is showing a name, home address, phone number, relatives, and a map to the front door — to anyone with a search box. For most people that's unsettling. For someone in a contested legal matter, a high-conflict divorce, or a stalking situation, it's dangerous. Praxis finds every place that data lives, scores it by real-world risk, and makes it go away — with a real, named person handling it, not a faceless subscription.

Who this is for

Mass-market removal apps are automated and impersonal. Executive-protection firms serve the Fortune 500 at enterprise prices. Praxis serves that middle — especially family-law, high-conflict-divorce, and DV attorneys, and the clients their cases put at risk. Local, named, responsive, monthly.

The line we won't cross

This service removes the data of consenting subjects. Every engagement begins with a signed written authorization from the person whose data we handle — audit or Care Plan, no exceptions.

What we will never do: research opposing parties, surveil anyone, or build dossiers on people who haven't asked us to protect them. If an engagement drifts toward investigating a third party, we decline it. A privacy company that snoops for the right price isn't a privacy company — and sovereignty means the same rules protect everyone, including the people on the other side of your case.
